The Team

Antonio Costa

Founder of Cafe Noir

Born in Trieste - the heart of Italian coffee, Antonio has lived and breathed coffee working in his families coffee shop from a young age. He brought his experience & love of the craft over to London & now runs his own award winning coffee shop - Cafe Noir.

Leah Kelley

Co-Owner

Leah believed in Cafe Noir back in its humble beginnings & without her strategic thinking & planning, Cafe Noir would not be where it is today. Leah has helped expand & run Cafe Noir, which has led to the two additional Cafe Noir coffee shops in London.

Yui Subiyanto

Head Barista

Yui's enthusiasm & passion for coffee is infectious but don't let her young looks fool you, she is true master of the coffee game. Yui is always experimenting & has created many exciting drinks that have been featured specials of Cafe Noir.
